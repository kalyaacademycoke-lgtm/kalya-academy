Our Story

An Academy Built
for Tomorrow's
Leaders.

Nestled in the green highlands of Kericho County, Kalya Academy is a premier private boarding school serving primary and junior secondary learners. We combine Kenya's CBC curriculum with global best practices, fostering academic rigor, moral character, and creative exploration.

Our campus at Chemosot — 200m off the Kericho-Sotik Highway — sits in a serene, secure environment purpose-designed for focused learning and holistic growth.

Academics

Programs & Curriculum

A rich academic environment anchored in Kenya's CBC, enriched by specialist talent tracks that ignite every learner's unique strengths.

Kenyan Competency Based Curriculum (CBC)

Fully compliant and expertly delivered across all grades — integrating Literacy, Numeracy, Environmental Activity, Movement & Creative Arts, and all Junior Secondary learning areas.

Grade 1 – Grade 9

Coding & Robotics

Hands-on programming workshops and robotics kits building future engineers and tech innovators from an early age.

Music & Drama

Vocal training, instrument lessons, drama rehearsals, and Kenyan music festivals participation for every aspiring performer.

Skating

One of the very few primary schools in the region offering structured skating as a developmental and competitive sport track.

Chess

Strategic thinking and analytical reasoning developed through our dedicated chess club, competing in regional and national tournaments.

Competitive Sports

Football, athletics, swimming, volleyball, and basketball teams with dedicated coaches targeting county and national championships.

Debate & Leadership

Student councils, prefectship, and inter-school debate programs building confident, articulate young leaders ready for the world.
